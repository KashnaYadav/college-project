Bohemian Macrame Wall Hanging

Kashna Yadav June 15, 2023 2 hours Home Decor

What You'll Need

  • Macrame cord (5mm, 50 yards)
  • Wooden dowel (24 inches)
  • Scissors
  • Tape measure
  • Comb (for fringe)
  • Optional: Wooden beads

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Prepare Your Materials

Cut 8 pieces of macrame cord, each 10 feet long. Fold each piece in half and attach to the wooden dowel using lark's head knots. You should have 16 cords hanging from the dowel.

2

Create the First Row of Knots

Divide the cords into 4 groups of 4 cords each. Starting with the first group, tie a square knot using the outer two cords around the center two cords. Repeat this for all 4 groups.

3

Add Variation with Alternating Knots

For the second row, create diagonal half-hitch knots by taking the outer left cord and tying half-hitch knots with each cord to its right. Repeat on the right side with the outer right cord.

4

Create the Diamond Pattern

Form groups of 4 cords again and tie square knots, but position them slightly lower than the first row to create a diamond pattern. Continue this pattern for 3-4 rows.

5

Finish with Fringe

Trim the bottom of your wall hanging to your desired length. Use a comb to separate the cords and create a soft fringe. Optionally, add wooden beads to some of the cords for extra texture.

Pro Tips

  • Keep your tension consistent for a uniform look
  • Use a clipboard to secure your work while knotting
  • For a fuller look, use more cords or a thicker cord
  • Experiment with different knot patterns for unique designs
