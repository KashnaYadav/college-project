Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit my own tutorial?

We love featuring community tutorials! Please send us an email with details about your project, along with photos and step-by-step instructions.

Do you offer video tutorials?

Yes! We're gradually adding video versions of our most popular tutorials. You can find them on our YouTube channel linked in the footer.

Can I request a specific tutorial?

Absolutely! We're always looking for new ideas. Use the contact form to suggest a tutorial topic you'd like to see on Craftify.

Do you sell craft supplies?

We don't sell supplies directly, but we often include links to recommended products in our tutorials. We're committed to suggesting quality, accessible materials.
